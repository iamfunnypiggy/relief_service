About Covid Releif Box

Covid Releif Box is a startup that partners with companies, governement, and individuals to provide covid releif packages during the pandemic. The covid releif packages are given out depending on the applicants needs.

Eligibility

The Covid Relief Box provides financial and material support to people in need during the global pandemic. Please review the eligibility criterea to see if you are able to apply.
Depending on your conditions, you may receive a different relief box
To be eligible for the Covid relief box, one is required to meet at least one of the following conditions

Conditions

  1. Living in a household with children under 18
  2. Living in a household with elderly
  3. Yearly Financial income under $41,000
  4. Currently supporting a dependant relative who lives in the same household
  5. Student enrolled at a university or college
  6. Small businewss owner (less than 100 employees)
  7. Lost employment due to Covid - 19
  8. Suffering from a health condition susceptible to COVID-19

Susceptible health conditions

  1. Asthma
  2. Cerebrovascular disease
  3. Cystic fibrosis
  4. Hypertension
  5. Immunocompromised state
  6. Dementia)
  7. Liver disease
  8. Overweight (BMI > 25)
  9. Puimonary fibrosis
  10. Thalassemia
  11. Type 1 diabetes
